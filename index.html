A Brief Biography...

Rachael spent the first decade of her life climbing over the San Gabrial mountains of Southern California, before moving to Fresno,the heart of California's breadbasket. She summited Mt. San Antonio, a 10,000+ peak, at the age of 9. Hiking through the Sierras created new oppportunities to challenge herself just as the beauty of the Sierras inspired her heart. She started her path as an artist at an early age dabling in sculpture, beadcraft, watercolors, story-telling, music, and any other media that drew and called to her.

Exploring both her desire to continue making art and wanting to do so in a sustainable way lead her to explore digital forms of her favorite media. Early exposure to coding in languages like BASIC and Pascal dovetailed to lead her to the intersection of art and coding, artistic web-development.
